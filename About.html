How it all started

There was need for a good savings culture to make provisions for the rainy seasons, there was also a dire need for easy access to funds when needed. Prior to the birth of the cooperative society, pioneer members of the society had been involved in several kinds of thrift contribution but there was need for something more. That was when the idea of cooperative society came up and it was embraced by all. Ever since then, it has been a great ride.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to help people develop a good savings culture and make credit facilities easily accessible for society members.

Our Vision

To be the number one solution to every financial needs across the nation

Meet The Board

The President

A graduate of Biochemistry at the Yaba College of Technology, Yaba Lagos. He holds a post graduate diploma in quality management with the Chartered Quality Institute and has worked in the capacity of quality manager for The Bridge Clinic. He has consulted for other Private Health Institutions in Nigeria before joining Reddington Multi-Specialist hospital. He is a Certified Provisional Lead Auditor with the Professional Evaluation and Certification Board after completing the required certification examination.

The Secretary

A graduate of Textile Technology at Yaba College of Technology and Accounting at the University of Lagos. He is a certified banker and programmer with vast knowledge of several programming languages. He is a freelance financial auditor and he currently works as a Banker at Fidelity Bank Plc.
